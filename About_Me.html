My Story

My journey began in Afghanistan, where pursuing an education required perseverance in the face of extraordinary challenges. When opportunities for girls became increasingly limited, I continued learning through online education while helping other girls continue theirs.

I taught English, mentored students, and supported community-based learning initiatives because I believed that education should remain accessible, even in the most difficult circumstances. Those experiences shaped my resilience and inspired my lifelong commitment to expanding educational opportunities for others.

Today, I am pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at Albukhary International University, where I am building a strong foundation in computing and preparing to specialize in Data Science. I am particularly interested in research, full-stack development, and educational technology.

Through academic projects, cross-cultural collaboration, and initiatives like STEM_CORE, I aspire to create innovative solutions that expand access to education, advance gender equity in STEM, and contribute to sustainable development.
